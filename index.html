Corruption Protection

In the year 20XX rampant government bribes has allowed for the creation of several mega toxic waste dumps. Radioactives, heavy metals, bio-hazards, nanoplastics, and more are discarded at these locations. One such dump is the Green Yard. A spill has occurred while transporting a medley of deadly materials there.
...
A sanitation crew sent to clean it up. You are the experimental nanobots running throughout their bodies protecting them from certain death as they trek deeper and deeper into the spill zone. Good luck.
...
Controls:


Select a building with your keyboard then click where you want to place it. A green outline will appear if it is placeable. Click it again to place.

1 - Generator - 5 Bio, 5 Metal, 5 Plastic: It passively generates bio material, some metal, and a little plastic from the host. It also periodically creates drones to retrieve material from slain invaders.

2 - Turret - 3 Bio, 1 Metal: Fires medium damage laser to the closest invader in its range.

3 - Energy Field - 2 Bio, 2 Metal: Short range, high damage to any invader in its radius.

4 - Wall - 1 Plastic: Blocks invaders. High health.

5 - Delete - Destroys a building (non-generators). Careful! No refunds.

Select and click again on a turret, energy field, or wall to upgrade it for 2 of each material. This fully heals the building as well.
- Turrets gain the ability to shoot another two beams if there are enough enemies in range along with a minor health boost.
- Energy fields do increased damage and have increased health.
- Walls gain greatly increased health.

For every multiple of 50 your score hits you gain a scaling bonus to all materials.

The infection starts once you place your first generator and ends once all generators are gone. Remember to leave enough materials to build a generator when starting the game.
